## 🎨 **DESIGN ADAPTATIONS**

### **🔹 Text Sizes**
- **Mobile:** `text-xs` to `text-sm`
- **Tablet:** `text-sm` to `text-base`
- **Desktop:** `text-base` to `text-lg`

### **🔹 Spacing**
- **Mobile:** `p-2` to `p-4`
- **Tablet:** `p-3` to `p-5`
- **Desktop:** `p-4` to `p-6`

### **🔹 Icons**
- **Mobile:** `w-4 h-4` to `w-5 h-5`
- **Tablet:** `w-5 h-5` to `w-6 h-6`
- **Desktop:** `w-6 h-6` to `w-8 h-8`

### **🔹 Buttons**
- **Mobile:** `py-2 px-4` with `text-sm`
- **Tablet:** `py-2.5 px-5` with `text-base`
- **Desktop:** `py-3 px-6` with `text-lg`
